Privacy Policy for DripFit

Last Updated: 23.12.2025

DripFit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use DripFit, we collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Username and Display Name: Your chosen identifier within the app
  • Profile Photo: If you choose to upload one (optional)

User-Generated Content

  • Outfit Photos: Images you upload to share with friends or publicly
  • Post Titles: Descriptions you add to your outfit posts
  • Comments: Feedback you provide on friends' posts
  • Votes: Your yes/no votes on outfit posts

Social Information

  • Friends List: Connections you make with other users
  • Friend Requests: Sent and received connection requests

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (pages visited, features used)
  • Log Data: Error logs and crash reports for troubleshooting

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the DripFit service
  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable you to upload and share outfit photos
  • Connect you with friends within the app
  • Display votes and comments on posts
  • Send you notifications about friend requests and activity
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Respond to your support requests

3. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following ways:

With Other Users

  • Public Posts: If you choose "Public" audience, anyone can see your post
  • Friends-Only Posts: Only your friends can see posts marked as "Friends"
  • Profile Information: Your username and display name are visible to other users

With Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database and authentication provider (PostgreSQL with encryption)
  • Cloud Storage: For storing uploaded photos securely

For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Investigate potential fraud or security issues

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/SSL) and at rest
  • Database: PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS)
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing (never stored in plain text)
  • Access Control: Strict permissions ensure users can only access their own data

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

Access and Update

  • View and edit your profile information within the app (tap the edit icon)
  • Update your display name and username anytime

Delete Your Data

  • Delete individual posts you've created
  • Remove friends from your crew
  • Delete your account (email us at dan@dwperformancelab.com)

Control Your Audience

  • Choose "Friends" or "Public" for each post
  • Unfriend users anytime
  • Decline friend requests

Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data by emailing dan@dwperformancelab.com

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Specifically:

  • Active Posts: Until you delete them or they expire (auto-expire after set time)
  • Account Data: Until you request account deletion
  • Deleted Posts: Marked as deleted in database (not displayed to users)
  • After Account Deletion: Data is permanently removed within 30 days

7. Children's Privacy

DripFit is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ. By using DripFit, you consent to this transfer.

9. Third-Party Links

DripFit may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Sending you a notification within the app (for major changes)

Your continued use of DripFit after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request what personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • Delete personal information held by us
  • Opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Email: dan@dwperformancelab.com
  • App: DripFit Settings → Support
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours
